About Me

Hello and welcome to my blog! My name is Sethe and is traditionally pronounced like "Seth-ee". However, I go by my nickname, which is just pronounced like "Seth". Although my name is Lithuanian, Seth has roots in Ancient Egyptian and Hebrew Languages. Set was the Egyptian God of blood, war, deserts, storms, and confusion. He murdered his own brother, Osiris. Seth was also the third son of Adam and Eve, born after Cain killed Abel. Unsuprisingly, the name Seth translates to "Blood child" in Hebrew!

I am a microbiology major and greatly enjoy working in the lab. I am currently most interested in prion diseases and would like to pursue a career researching them. Some of the most famous prion diseases are mad cow disease (bonvine spongiform encephalopathy), and kuru (Cretsfeldt-Jakob disease).
